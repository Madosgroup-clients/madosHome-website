Features

Nearby

Easily explore differents nearby properties based on your current location.

Search

We offer you an efficient search. Thanks to the relevant data that we ask for, you will find the property of your search.

Publish

We offer you the possibility to have up to 3 properties publicly visible on our platform for free. To have more than 3 publicly visible properties, you will need to subscribe to our platform.

Save favotites

To make your visit to our platform enjoyable, we offer you the possibility of having a favorite box where you can store your favorite properties.
