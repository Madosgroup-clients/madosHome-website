Features

Near By

We provide you with a map view that will allow you to easily explore the different properties based on your current location.

Search

We offer you an efficient search. Thanks to the relevant data that we ask for in your search. you can have a precise search that will offer you the house of your dreams.

Publish

The platform doesn't just give you the view. but also you can add your property in our platform with or without subscription. To benefit from the possibility of making your property visible, you must have a subscription.

Save favotites

In order to make your visit to our platform pleasant, we offer you to have a favorite properties box where you can store your favorite properties.
