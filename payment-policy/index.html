Payment policies

1. Credit and debit card

We use third-party services for payment processing. We will not store or collect your payment card details. That information is provided directly to our third-party payment processors whose use of your personnal information is governed by their privacy policy . Our payment processsor adhere to the standards set by PCI-DSS as managed by the PCI Security Standards Council, which is a joint effort of brands like Visa, Mastercard, American Express and Discover. PCI-DSS requirements help ensure the secure handling of payment information.


2. Bank transfer

To activate your subscription, please transfer the sum corresponding to the selected subscription plan to our payment agent quoting as the name of the sender the reference number that will be generated by our platform. The reference number is valid for 24 hours, at which time payment is expected. If the reference number is not included in the notes with the transfer, we will not be able to activate your subscription. This includes the irreversibility of the operation, hence the loss of your money.

The process of activating your subscription may take up to 7 business days.
